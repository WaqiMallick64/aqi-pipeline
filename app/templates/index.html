Project Overview

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a measure of how polluted the air is and what associated health effects might be a concern for the general public. Monitoring AQI is crucial for public health, environmental protection, and informed decision-making. Our system leverages machine learning to forecast AQI based on weather and pollution data.

Data Source

Utilizes real-time weather and pollution data from reliable APIs to train and predict AQI levels.

Machine Learning

Employs trained regression models to analyze patterns and provide accurate AQI predictions.

Forecast Insights

Delivers next 3-day AQI forecasts with insights to help users plan and stay healthy.
